TRADEMARK SPECIALTIES

Maybe you’ve already heard about our 24/7 $4 EL CHEAPO margaritas, made with fresh pressed S&S, available all day, every day.  The same goes for “the hat,” our $5 Jim Beam shots, the perfect accessory for your beer.  Or “the sombrero,” our $5 El Jimador shots, available any time.  A full menu for each location is available in our menu section.  Menus:  Pike Place Market and Ballard.
